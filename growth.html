Who it is for

Use Growth when one good opportunity is worth doing outbound properly.

This is not mass lead generation. It works best when the buyer is specific, the sale needs a real conversation and the wrong message would damage the brand.

Best fit

  • Professional services, advisory, consulting and accounting firms
  • SaaS, technology, managed IT and B2B software companies
  • Recruitment, staffing and talent businesses
  • Business services, finance, property, construction, healthcare and education niches

Poor fit

  • Low-ticket offers where one meeting cannot justify the work
  • Broad consumer audiences with no clear decision-maker
  • Offers that are still too unclear to explain in one conversation
  • Teams looking for a cheap list rather than a serious outbound partner

Why it is different

Most outbound breaks because too many people own different pieces.

Typical setup

  • Strategy discussed once, then handed to delivery
  • A rented list used as the campaign foundation
  • Messages written before the buyer is properly understood
  • Reporting focused on sends, opens and activity
  • Client left to connect the dots between tools and people

AA Growth

  • Buyer thinking, data, messaging and outreach managed together
  • Decision-maker data built around your specific buyer
  • Message angles shaped by the buyer's industry and role
  • Reporting focused on replies, conversations, learning and next actions
  • Human team accountable for the operating rhythm and weekly decisions
