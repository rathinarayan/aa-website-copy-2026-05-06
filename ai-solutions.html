AI Solutions

Practical AI for work your team should not keep doing manually.

AI Solutions is separate from Growth. It is for internal admin, documents, reporting, sorting requests and repeatable work that slows the team down.

Start a fit conversationSee examples

Simple distinction

Growth uses AI to help AA run outbound. AI Solutions builds AI your team can use inside your own business.

One helps us reach buyers for you. The other helps your team save time on internal work.

How we judge fit

We look for work that repeats often enough to be worth fixing.

Good fit

Work that keeps repeating.

Heavy on reading or writing Document review, summaries, report drafting, customer support routing.
Repeats the same way Order checks, intake decisions, quote preparation, CRM updates.
Worth the effort The work takes enough time, causes enough delay or creates enough risk to justify building something properly.

Poor fit

Work that does not need AI.

No clear owner If nobody owns the process, automation will not fix it.
No repeat pattern If every case is completely different, AI will not help much.
Too small to matter If a process change or spreadsheet solves it, we will say so before recommending AI.

Examples

Common places AI can remove manual work.

1

Document review assistant

For a law, finance or advisory team reviewing long documents and extracting risk, clauses, dates or next actions.

2

Support request sorting

For an operations or support team that needs to sort requests, draft replies and send work to the right person.

3

Supplier ordering assistant

For a multi-site business managing repeat ordering, approvals, exceptions and stock issues.

4

Internal reporting system

For a leadership team that needs weekly reporting pulled from multiple sources without manual copy-paste.
