How the team is built

One company. Two offices. One payroll.

AA is a 100 per cent Australian-owned company with its own office and team in India. Melbourne runs the relationship and strategy. AA-owned India runs delivery. Both teams are on the AA payroll, under AA management, held to the AA standard. Everyone who touches the work sits inside AA.

Melbourne

Account management · Strategy · Founders

Every client gets a named Melbourne-based account manager from day one. The same person every week. Not a rotating pool, not a ticket queue. They run strategy, report progress, pick up the phone when you have a question and stay accountable with you.

Narayan and the founding team also sit in Melbourne, and stay involved on important client decisions.

AA-owned operations · India

Delivery · outbound team · AI build team

AA owns its own office and team in India. Our own lease, our own payroll, our own hiring standards. They run the campaigns, build the AI and deliver the work. One company, two offices, one standard.

Melvin leads India operations, owning daily delivery quality and team rhythm. The outbound team and AI build team sit inside AA-owned structure.
